Craving Proposal

This is a very simple design to help you communicate with your friends and discuss where to eat.

Following these steps to use Craving Proposal
1 Find a dish you are craving for.
2 Add it to the Craving Proposal.
3 Repeat step 1 and 2 until you have a few options.
4 Open Craving Proposal and save it to generate a unique URL.
5 Send the URL to your friends and ask them to vote.
