Craving Proposal

A simple design to help you communicate with your friends and made decision on where to eat with a group.

Following these steps to use Craving Proposal
1 Find a dish you are craving for.
2 Add it to the Craving Proposal.
3 Repeat step 1 and 2 until you have a few options.
4 Open Craving Proposal and send the proposal uinique URL to your friends.
5 Your friends will vote and make decision together.
